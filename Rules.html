Kinder Kares Franchise Rules & Regulations

To acquire a Kinder Kares franchise, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum space requirement: 1500 sq. ft.
  • Located within 1000 meters of a hospital.
  • Fully air-conditioned facility with a minimum 2-ton AC capacity.
  • Safe and child-friendly infrastructure.
  • Compliance with local government regulations and licensing.
  • Qualified and trained staff for child care and emergency handling.
  • Basic first-aid and medical emergency setup.
  • Proper hygiene and sanitation facilities.
  • Minimum financial investment as per franchise agreement.

For further details, please contact us at info@kinderkares.com
